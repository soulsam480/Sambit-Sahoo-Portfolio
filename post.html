* Getting Started

12th July 2019

Hii , as you have came along all these way, I won't intend to introduce myself. It's my third attempt in a row to write a daily blog. I don't know if the will to write it regularly will last longer or not , But i will try my best to keep it continuous .


I intend to write this blog with honesty and sheer truthfullness . I have now reached a point in my life where lying won't help me anyway. I do know that saying the truth won't help anyway either, it will lead to various problems including conflicts with my friends and more. But what's the point in living a life where you don't say what you want to.This blog is going to be full of confessions and everything i always thought but never had the courage to say.


This blog is the outcome of a situation i faced a while ago. It's not something i'm doing out of anger or agony. This is something which should have been done a long ago. So let's hope foe the best and Thank you fo reading .
